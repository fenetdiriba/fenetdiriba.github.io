Project 3.1. Predicts whether a company will beat or miss Wall Street earnings estimates. Combines NLP sentiment analysis on earnings call transcripts with financial metrics. Group project for AI4ALL.

Two signals: what executives say on earnings calls (processed with FinBERT, a financial language model) and quantitative metrics like estimate revisions and past earnings history. Trained and validated on S&P 500 data.
